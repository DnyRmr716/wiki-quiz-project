The odd Wikipedia Quiz game!

How to Play

You'll be presented with a series of wikipedia articles and its your job to find out if they're Real or Fake. Click the "Real" button if you believe the statement is true, or the "Fake" button if you think it's false. You have 3 strikes. If you get 3 answers wrong, the game is over. Get through 15 questions, and you win!
